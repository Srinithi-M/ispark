About i-SPARK:

i-SPARK is a Quality Circle(QC) team consisting of 8 members.
They are volunteering members, who commit themselves for creating an awareness on
uses of internet. Their motto is "Take advantage over the technology..!" and their mantra is
"Remember, The World's in your hand...!". They use many tools and techniques to serve their purpose and some of them are:-

  • Questionnaire
  • Pareto graph
  • Check sheet
  • Bar graph
  • Radar chart
  • Pie chart
  • Gaunt chart
  • Plan-Do-Check-Act chart
  • Activity Plan Matrix
